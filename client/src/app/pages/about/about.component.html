Concept and Philosophy

The concept of the website is to let player make shop and offer items which will be displayed on the site.

Each player then can set his shop online to notify he is available to trade with. (the timer of 15min is arbitrary and not mandatory to get requests)

All the items remain visible for one week after their last onlining, and are displayed from newest to oldest in addition of from cheapest to most expensive.

Custom Policy

Any attempt to circumvent the principles of this site will be strictly prohibited and may result in appropriate action.

There is no place for scams or RMT here.
