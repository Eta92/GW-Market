Concept and philosophy

The concept of the website is to let players create shops and offer items which will be displayed on the site.

Each player can set their shop to online (highlighted) to communicate whether they are available for trading or not. (The timer of 15 min is arbitrary and not mandatory to get requests.)

All the items remain visible for one week after you last published your shop, and can be displayed from newest to oldest in addition of from cheapest to most expensive or alphabetically.

Custom policy

Any attempt to circumvent the principles of this site will be strictly prohibited and may result in appropriate action.

There is no place for scams or RMT here.
