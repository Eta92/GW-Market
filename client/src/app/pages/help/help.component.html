Quick help center

What is the goal of this site?

Give players an easy way to trade items via an organized marketplace. Let spamming in Kamadan be a thing of the past.

What do I need to do to get an item?

Type the name of the item in the search bar or browse the recent orders. Once you have found one with the right price, you can click a button to copy & paste a messages to the vendor in-game.

How do I set up my shop? How do I post orders?

No log-in or account is required. No data can be stolen. Simply create an item order, set your character name, and refresh your shop. To keep your shop data at hand regularly export your json file, so when cookies or the cache get cleared, you can simply reimport your stock.

What does being online mean for my shop?

Currently, it is an arbitrary 15 min timer during which your shop items are promoted the most. Don't worry, they will stay visible for one week, and are saved in your shop until you delete them.

What does certifying a character do?

To certify your character you may send an in-game message to a verification account. All it does is let the owner of the website know, that the character you registered actually exists. It is a scam protection. It is a recommended step also to help recover your shop if you clean your cache or use another device.

My character is already certified on another shop, what do I do?

You need to certify the same character once more, this will link your old shop to the new one and merge the content of both shops. If you use 2 devices, they should now be synced.

How do I get the green online badge?

You need to use a custom launcher named Daybreak and open the API in the options. This let the web-browser chat with your game. It also tracks your character switch automatically. Here is the Github page: https://github.com/gwdevhub/Daybreak
