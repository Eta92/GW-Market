Quick Help Center

What is the goal of this site ?

Allow an easy way to trade item via a marketplace to organize trade offers instead of spamming Kamadan

What do I need to do to get an item ?

Allow an easy way to trade item via a marketplace to organize trade offers instead of spamming Kamadan

What do I need to post orders ?

Basicaly nothing, you do not need any account, just create an item order, set you character name, and refresh your shop.

What does it mean to be online for my shop ?

It is an arbitrary 15min time while your shop items are promoted the most. Don't worry they will stay visible one week, and stay in your shop until you delete them.

What is the use to certify characters ?

It is a recommended step to let player know your shop is legit, also it bound your shop to this player so it helps to recover it if you clean your cache or use another device.

My character is already certified on another shop, what to do ?

You need to certify the same character once more, this will link your old shop to the new one and merge both content. If you use 2 devices they should now be synced.

How do I get the green online badge ?

You need to use a custom launcher named Daybreak and open the API in the options. This let the web-browser to chat with your game. It also makes your character switch automatically.
